Instructions

  1. Requirement: Select New if you require to apply fresh IEC Code. Select Modify if you need changes in existing IEC Code.
  2. Name of Business Entity: Fill the Business Entity which will get printed on IEC Certificate.
  3. Constitution of Busines: Select business type of the applicant in which name IEC requires to apply.
  4. Description of Business: Fill the name of Business / Enterprise which will get printed on IEC Certificate.
  5. Business Activity: Select main business activity to be conducted.
  6. Date of Incorporation / Date of Birth: Select Your Date of Incorporation / Date of Birth.

Frequently Asked Question (IEC)

What is IEC?

IEC stands for International Electrotechnical Commission, a global organization that prepares and publishes international standards for all electrical, electronic, and related technologies.

How are standards developed?

Standards are developed through a collaborative process involving technical experts, industry representatives, and other stakeholders. The process typically involves research, consensus-building, and public review.

Importance of IEC Standards

IEC standards ensure safety, reliability, and interoperability of electrical and electronic products and systems across different countries and industries.

Global Reach

IEC works with 171 countries, representing 98% of the world's population and more than 96% of global electricity generation.

Technical Committees

IEC has over 200 technical committees and subcommittees that cover various domains like power generation, transmission, and electrical safety.

Certification Systems

IEC provides certification systems that help manufacturers demonstrate compliance with international standards and facilitate global trade.
